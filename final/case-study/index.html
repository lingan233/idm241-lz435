Example Description

Trigger

  • Click on the area that includes the hamburger menu button and the text “Menu”

Rules

  • A menu modal showed up from the top right corner of the screen
  • The background of the menu slides out from the right
  • The middle line of the hamburger menu button rotates clockwise 405 degrees
  • The top and bottom line of the hamburger menu disappear after the middle line of the hamburger pass over them
  • Another line fade in perpendicular to the middle line forming an X
  • The menu items fade in
  • The menu text fade out quickly
  • The close text fade in quickly

Feedbacks

  • A menu modal showed up from the top right corner of the screen
  • The background of the menu slides out from the right
  • The middle line of the hamburger menu button rotates clockwise 405 degrees
  • The top and bottom line of the hamburger menu disappear after the middle line of the hamburger pass over them
  • Another line fade in perpendicular to the middle line forming an X
  • The menu items fade in
  • The menu text fade out quickly
  • The close text fade in quickly

Loops & Modes

  • No loops
  • There will be a menu modal close mode and a menu modal open mode. The menu modal close mode is the default. The menu open mode will be carried out by clicking on the hamburger menu. The menu close mode will be restored by clicking on the close button

Code/Dev

  • The menu open and close part is taken from code pen
  • Select and deselect option are figured out through googling
