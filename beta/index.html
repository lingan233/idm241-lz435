Beta Description

Triggers

  • Actions that occurred in the previous alpha part will trigger the beta part of the project. The summary of the alpha part is described below:
    Click on the button --> The button will turn into a rounded edge horizontal straight line. The text and the icon fade out while the button decrease in size.

Rules

  • An envelope fade in from above the existing horizontal dark blue bar, rotating counter-clockwise, moving toward the bottom left relatively
  • The envelope disappears after overlapping with the blue bar
  • An email is sent
  • The blue bar shrink in width
  • 4 different small circles emitted outward in 4 different directions from under the blue bar and fade out
